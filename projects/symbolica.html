Symbolica

Symbolica is a world where precious gems are used to create powerful symbols that hold the key to the kingdom's prosperity and security. However, a wicked sorcerer known as the Shadow Mage has stolen the gems and uses them to enslave the kingdom.

Symbolica is a year-long individual project. I am always very critical about many sides of the experience provided by GDD. One aspect is that most of the class projects feel like practices. After having a very bad experience with randomly assigned teammates in board game class due to the vision differences. I decided to create a real game that is eventually publishable in my way and by myself.

Every week I go to Crash Test which is a half offical event where people bring board games and have some playtests. It took me a whole year to develop the game and eventually I feel like it is publishable.
