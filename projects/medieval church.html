Medieval Church

Composing for game is best course I took in RIT. Thanks to the Eastman School of Music in Rochester, all my music teachers are very patient and motivating.

I spent a lot of time to make music in that semester and my instructor Tom Davis is the one makes those time worthwhile. I don't come from a music background and the class is surprisingly not that hard. I learned a lot of things. I used Ableton Live to created music and intergrated it into a Unity scene using Wwise. Wwise is the game industry's standard software to deal with sound. I am very satified with the learning outcome and my final project.

This learning experience tells a lot about what makes learning joyful. 1. The content should be highly related to the learner and be useful. 2. Feedbacks are provided in a meaningful and instant way. 3. Everything should be clear, which requires teacher's through understanding of the content.
